Customer Service Standards Policy

Purpose

This policy establishes customer service standards that all employees and trainees must follow to ensure a consistently positive experience for every guest. These standards are designed to promote Villa Roma Hotel’s reputation for exceptional hospitality and guest satisfaction.

Scope

This policy applies to all Villa Roma Hotel employees and trainees, particularly those in guest-facing roles. It encompasses all interactions, including in-person, phone, email, and social media communications.

Policy Guidelines

Core Customer Service Values

  • Courtesy and Respect: Treat all guests with respect, courtesy, and professionalism.
  • Attentiveness: Listen actively to guests’ needs, preferences, and feedback. Respond promptly to questions and concerns.
  • Personalized Service: Recognize and acknowledge guest preferences whenever possible, delivering a tailored experience.

Greeting and Interactions

  • Warm Welcomes: Greet guests promptly with a friendly smile and an appropriate greeting.
  • Use of Names: Address guests by name whenever possible to add a personal touch.
  • Polite Communication: Use polite language, such as “please” and “thank you,” maintaining a calm, friendly tone.

Responsiveness and Problem-Solving

  • Timely Response: Address guest inquiries or requests immediately, following up within 10 minutes or updating the guest.
  • Problem Resolution: Resolve issues quickly by offering solutions or alternatives to meet guest needs.
  • Apology and Ownership: Apologize genuinely if a service does not meet guest expectations, taking ownership and corrective action.

Service Standards by Area

  • Front Desk: Ensure efficient, friendly check-ins and check-outs, providing information on amenities and local attractions.
  • Food and Beverage: Serve food and drinks promptly, checking in on guests’ dining experience, and accommodating dietary needs.
  • Housekeeping: Maintain cleanliness in all rooms and common areas, ensuring guest rooms are clean, stocked, and welcoming.
  • Concierge Services: Offer knowledgeable recommendations, make reservations, and assist with guest inquiries to enhance their stay.

Communication Standards

  • Clear and Positive Language: Use language that is clear, positive, and easy to understand, avoiding jargon.
  • Professional Body Language: Maintain open body language, making eye contact and avoiding closed-off gestures.
  • Follow-Up: Follow up with guests after providing service to confirm satisfaction and address further needs.

Handling Difficult Situations

  • Stay Calm and Patient: Remain calm and patient, allowing frustrated or upset guests to express their concerns.
  • Empathy and Understanding: Show empathy, letting guests know their concerns are heard and understood.
  • Immediate Action: Take immediate steps to resolve issues, informing guests of escalation if necessary.

Continuous Improvement

  • Guest Feedback: Encourage feedback through comment cards, surveys, or direct conversation, using it to improve services.
  • Team Training: Participate in regular training to reinforce customer service skills and stay updated on Villa Roma’s latest standards.

Compliance and Accountability

  • Monitoring: Supervisors will regularly monitor interactions to ensure adherence to these standards.
  • Performance Reviews: Customer service performance will be reviewed periodically, with corrective action for non-compliance.

Policy Review and Updates

This Customer Service Standards Policy will be reviewed annually to ensure it aligns with guest expectations and industry best practices. Updates will be communicated to all employees and trainees.

Acknowledgment

All employees and trainees must read, understand, and acknowledge this policy, confirming their commitment to uphold Villa Roma’s customer service standards.
